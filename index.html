Australian Energy Data Overview

Welcome to the Australian Energy Data visualisation platform. This project presents an interactive exploration of energy consumption trends, rainfall, and renewable energy production across Australia's states and territories. Dive into maps and charts showcasing real-time data from the 2021-22 fiscal year.

Energy Consumption by State

This map illustrates energy consumption by state along with additional data on rainfall and dam storage levels. Hover over the markers to see details for each state.

Energy Consumption by Industry

This bar chart presents energy consumption across different industries from 2008 to 2023. Compare how various sectors like agriculture, manufacturing, and transport have utilized energy over the years.

Renewable Energy Consumption

This line chart showcases renewable energy consumption across Australia from 2015 to 2023. Track the growth of various energy sources like solar, wind, biomass, and hydropower over time.

Rainfall and Dam Storage Levels

Here, we compare rainfall levels with dam storage capacities for different regions of Australia from 2014 to 2022. This visualization highlights how rainfall correlates with water storage in major dams across the country.
